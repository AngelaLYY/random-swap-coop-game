Random Swap Co-op

A cozy two-player chase game made for long-distance couples. One runs, one chases—then your roles swap at random. Stay close even when you’re far apart.

How to play

  • Runner — Collect the green dots before you get tagged.
  • Chaser — Tag the runner to score.
  • Random swap — Your roles switch at random during the round. Watch and listen for the “RUN!” or “CHASE!” cue.
  • Up to 3 rounds per match. The game ends when the match is over.
